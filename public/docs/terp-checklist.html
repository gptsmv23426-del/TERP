What You'll Need for Your TERP Application

These are the documents and information TCEQ typically requires. Gather what you can, and Justin will help fill in the gaps.

Business & Tax Information

  • W-9 (Request for Taxpayer ID Number) Completed and signed. Must match your business entity.
  • Business entity documentation LLC filing, DBA, or sole proprietor documentation as applicable.

Old Equipment (the machine you're retiring)

  • Equipment details Make, model, year, serial number, horsepower, engine tier.
  • Proof of ownership Title, registration, or bill of sale showing you own the machine.
  • Photos of the equipment Clear photos showing the machine, the serial number plate, and the engine.
  • Hour meter reading Photo of the hour meter or best estimate of annual operating hours.
  • Operating location County (or counties) where the machine primarily operates. Must be 75%+ in eligible counties.

New Equipment (the replacement)

  • Quote or purchase details Dealer quote for the replacement machine, if you have one. Justin can help with this.
  • Replacement equipment specs Make, model, year, horsepower, engine tier of the machine you plan to buy.

Other

  • Any existing TCEQ correspondence If you've applied before or received letters from TCEQ about TERP.
  • Fleet information (if applicable) Total number of machines you own. Owners of 5 or fewer may qualify for a small-business set-aside.

This checklist is for preparation only. Requirements may vary by program and grant round. TCEQ determines all final documentation requirements. Justin will walk you through what applies to your specific situation.
